**Topic**: Indication-Why the procedure is being done

**Question**: Based on the consent text, why might a cystoscopy be performed?
A) To treat discomfort during urination
B) To diagnose a potential infection
C) To stop bleeding
D) The consent text does not provide information on why a cystoscopy is performed

**Answer**: D
**Rationale**: The consent text only mentions the risks of the procedure (bleeding, infection, and discomfort during urination), but does not provide any information on why a cystoscopy might be performed.
